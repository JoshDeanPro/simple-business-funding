# Prompt: Document Collection Follow-up Email

This template is used by the file coordinator to request missing, incomplete, or corrupted document files (specifically business bank statements or current funding agreements) needed to complete the underwriting package.

---

## Template Metadata
*   **Sender**: lizzy.alemayehu@smallbizloanz.com
*   **Trigger**: Application submitted without statements, or statements provided are unreadable/incomplete.
*   **Goal**: Secure six consecutive months of recent business bank statements in PDF format.

---

## Subject Lines (Select One)
1.  Action required: Documents needed for [Business Name]
2.  Missing bank statements for SmallBizLoans file — [Business Name]
3.  Document update needed: SmallBizLoans representative Lizzy

## Email Body

Hi [Owner Name],

I am currently reviewing your file for [Business Name] to submit to Mom & Pop Business Funding. 

To complete your package, the underwriters require **six consecutive months of your most recent business bank statements**. 

Currently, we are missing the statements for:
*   [List missing months, e.g., March, April, May]

### How to Submit Your Statements:
1.  **Reply Directly**: You can attach the PDF statements directly to this email in reply.
2.  **Ensure Formatting**: Please provide complete statements (all pages, including blank pages) in PDF format. We cannot accept screenshots of transactions or online summaries.

Once I receive these statements, I will finalize your package and send it to the underwriting desk for review.

If you have any questions or have trouble downloading your statements from your online banking portal, please let me know or call me at **(720) 900-1921**.

## Coordinator Notes
*   Ensure the statements are from the *business bank account* matching the legal name or DBA of the business. Personal bank statements cannot be used.
*   Ensure that all pages of the statement PDFs are included to prevent underwriters from rejecting the file.
