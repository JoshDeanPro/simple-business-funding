# Prompt: Initial Coordinator Outreach Email

This template is used by the file coordinator (e.g., Lizzy Alemayehu) to contact the business owner immediately after they submit their initial application form.

---

## Template Metadata
*   **Sender**: lizzy.alemayehu@smallbizloanz.com
*   **Trigger**: Application form successfully submitted on the website.
*   **Goal**: Acknowledge receipt, establish contact, and schedule a brief verification phone call.

---

## Subject Lines (Select One)
1.  Reviewing your SmallBizLoans file — [Business Name]
2.  Next steps for your business funding file — [Business Name]
3.  Application received: SmallBizLoans representative Lizzy

---

## Email Body

Hi [Owner Name],

Thank you for submitting your business details to SmallBizLoans. I have received your information and am currently preparing your file to submit to the underwriting desk at Mom & Pop Business Funding.

To move your file forward, we need to verify a few details and ensure we have all required documents.

### What We Need Next:
1.  **A 5-Minute Call**: We need to quickly verify your business address, tax ID, and ownership structure.
2.  **Recent Bank Statements**: If you did not attach your six consecutive months of business bank statements during submission, please reply directly to this email with the PDF files.

Are you available for a brief phone call today or tomorrow? Please let me know a time that works best for you, or call me directly at **(720) 900-1921**.

Best regards,

**Lizzy Alemayehu**  
File Coordinator, SmallBizLoans  
Phone: (720) 900-1921  
Email: lizzy.alemayehu@smallbizloanz.com  

---

## Coordinator Notes
*   *Do not* promise approval amounts, rates, or terms.
*   *Do not* state that the file is "pre-qualified" or "pre-approved."
*   If the applicant carries an active cash advance balance, ask them to provide their original contract details during the phone call.
