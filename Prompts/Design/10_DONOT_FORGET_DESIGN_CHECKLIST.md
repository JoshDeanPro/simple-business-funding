# Prompt: Visual Design Quality Control Checklist

Use this prompt to verify page hierarchy and branding rules before finishing a task.

---

## "Don't Forget" Visual QA Checklist

*   [ ] **Navigation & Header**:
    *   Does the header transition smoothly from transparent to solid white upon scroll?
    *   Are logo coordinates readable on both dark hero backgrounds and light subpages?
    *   Does the mobile hamburger icon toggle states cleanly without shifting text?
*   [ ] **Color System**:
    *   Is Clear Cobalt used as the primary action color?
    *   Is Signal Lime restricted to checks, select states, and title accents?
    *   Is deep forest green or red excluded from general text blocks?
*   [ ] **Spacing & Grids**:
    *   Are vertical grids set to `py-16 sm:py-20` spacing?
    *   Is there a minimum of `gap-6` on form field blocks?
    *   Does the body canvas remain inside `max-w-6xl` guidelines?
*   [ ] **Forms**:
    *   Do form inputs render white background fills?
    *   Is the validation focus ring visible?
