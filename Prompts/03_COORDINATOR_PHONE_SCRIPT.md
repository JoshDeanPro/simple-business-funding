# Prompt: Coordinator Phone Verification Script

This script guides the file coordinator during the initial 5-minute phone call to verify applicant details, set expectations, and request missing documents.

---

## Script Metadata
*   **Call Goal**: Verify business information, assess current funding needs, explain the relationship to Mom & Pop Business Funding, and set expectations for the review process.
*   **Target Duration**: 3 to 5 minutes.
*   **Tone**: Professional, helpful, transparent, grounded.

---

## 1. The Opening
> "Hello, may I speak with [Owner Name]? 
> 
> Hi [Owner Name], this is Lizzy Alemayehu calling from SmallBizLoans. I received the business funding request you submitted online for [Business Name] and wanted to thank you for reaching out. 
> 
> I'm calling to quickly verify a few details so I can package your file and get it submitted to the review desk. Do you have about three minutes to go over this with me?"

*If the owner is busy:*
> "No problem at all. When would be a better time to call you back today, or would you prefer to schedule a time tomorrow?"

---

## 2. Information Verification
> "Perfect. I see here that the legal name of the business is [Legal Name], operating as [DBA if applicable]. Is that correct?
> 
> And are you still operating at [Physical Address]?
> 
> I also see you are requesting [Amount Requested] to help with [Merchant Type / Intent]. Can you tell me a little bit more about what you plan to address with these funds?"

---

## 3. Prior Funding Check (If Applicable)
*If they indicated carrying an active advance/loan balance:*
> "I see you mentioned you have an active funding balance with [Previous Company]. To package your file correctly, we will need to include your original agreement and your current balance. What is the current outstanding balance on that account, and what is your daily or weekly payment?"

---

## 4. Explaining the Relationship and Next Steps
> "Great, thank you. As a next step, I will submit this completed package to Mom & Pop Business Funding. They are the direct underwriters who will review your bank statements and make the funding decision.
> 
> [If statements are missing]: To submit the package, I will need you to email me your last six consecutive months of business bank statements. You can reply directly to the email I sent you earlier.
> 
> Once I submit your file with the statements, the review desk typically takes about one business day to determine what options are available. If an offer is generated, I will contact you immediately to review the terms together."

---

## 5. The Closing
> "Do you have any questions for me about the process or the documents we need?
> 
> Excellent. I'll look out for your bank statements, and as soon as I receive them, I'll get your file submitted. Thank you for your time, [Owner Name], and have a great rest of your day."
