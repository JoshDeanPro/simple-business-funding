# Step 4 Audit — Messaging and Ethical Conversion Specification

This specification documents the approved brand voice, terminology, final copy blocks, and microcopy to ensure SmallBizLoans is clear, credible, and compliant.

---

## 1. Brand Voice
*   **Professional**: Respectful of the owner's time and business history.
*   **Trustworthy**: Accurately representing that we coordinate with an underwriter rather than lending directly.
*   **Grounded**: Using concrete examples of expenses and operations instead of promises of growth or stress reduction.
*   **Human**: Written in plain, conversational English, avoiding corporate jargon.

---

## 2. Approved & Prohibited Terminology

| Approved Terminology | Prohibited Terminology | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "Submit application file" | "Get prequalified instantly" | Form submissions are manually reviewed; there is no automated scoring. |
| "Request coordinator review" | "Guaranteed approval / 100% success" | Approvals depend entirely on underwriting parameters. |
| "Prepare business statements" | "No paperwork required" | Six months of business bank statements are standard and necessary. |
| "Coordinate your submission" | "Immediate cash loan" | Focus on coordinator role rather than instant direct lending. |
| "Independent representative" | "Direct national lender / Bank" | SmallBizLoans acts strictly as a representative packaging files. |

---

## 3. Chosen Hero Copy Option
*   **Headline**: `Business funding for what comes next.`
*   **Supporting Copy**: *"Handle an immediate expense, purchase inventory or equipment, prepare for an opportunity, or create more room to operate. We help you complete and submit your application to Mom & Pop Business Funding."*
*   **Primary CTA Label**: `"Start My Application"`
*   **Secondary Action Label**: `"See What You'll Need"`
*   **Reassurance Line**: `"One straightforward application • Clear document requirements • Human representative follow-up"`

---

## 4. Homepage Section Copy

### Funding-Need Selector Question & Options
*   *Question*: `"What would you like your business to move forward on?"`
*   *Options*:
    *   `Immediate expense` -> *"Immediate bills, supplier cycles, or sudden machinery updates. We package your file for reviewer review."*
    *   `Inventory or equipment` -> *"Secure seasonal stock at bulk discounts or acquire machinery. Submit six months of statements to start."*
    *   `Growth opportunity` -> *"Expand to new operating spaces, secure commercial contracts, or fund client expansion operations."*
    *   `Hiring or capacity` -> *"Add support technicians, seasonal operators, or increase capability to handle larger orders."*
    *   `More breathing room` -> *"Build operational reserves or navigate historically slower months with flexible working capital."*

### Practical Business Outcomes
*   *Featured Card*: `"Managing inventory and operations"` — *"Keeping day-to-day operations moving is critical. Business owners utilize funding to purchase stock at bulk discounts, manage employee payroll cycles, cover supplier invoices, or handle unexpected machinery repairs. Maintaining operational rhythm prevents lost momentum."*
*   *Bullet Highlights*: Focus on supporting employees and planning operations instead of reacting to immediate hurdles.

### Three-Step Process
1.  `01 Complete the form`: *"Submit basic business details, owner contact information, and requested funding parameters via our secure form."*
2.  `02 Provide bank statements`: *"Provide six months of recent business bank statements. These are required to evaluate cash flow and verify business activity."*
3.  `03 Review with coordinator`: *"A representative will contact you directly to discuss the file details, coordinate with the funder, and explain options."*

### Preparation Copy
*   List requirements cleanly: Physical business address, tax ID (EIN), consecutive business statements, active contact lines.

### Role Explanation
*   *Heading*: `"How SmallBizLoans helps"`
*   *Wording*: *"SmallBizLoans helps business owners prepare and submit applications to Mom & Pop Business Funding. We are an independent representative, not a bank. Mom & Pop Business Funding reviews submitted applications and determines whether funding options are available."*

---

## 5. Application Microcopy

*   **Before you begin**: *"Completing the online form takes approximately 5 minutes. Six consecutive months of recent business bank statements will be requested to complete the file review."*
*   **Why ownership information is requested**: *"Ownership and identity information is requested to verify business files and obtain credit or background reports as allowed by law."*
*   **Why revenue information is requested**: *"Evaluating gross and card sales volume allows underwriters to assess operational cash flow."*
*   **Prior-funding conditional statement**: *"If your business carries an active balance, please indicate the original balance and current repayment rate."*
*   **Submission Success**: *"Application received. Thank you. Your application has been submitted successfully. A representative will review your information and contact you regarding the next steps."*
